Prototype chart. Click a segment to play a stub audio file.

All consonants are [-syllabic], so this feature is not included in the chart.

Hayes Vowel Features

Prototype chart. Click a vowel to play a stub audio file.

The basic features for vowels (shown by the basic IPA symbols rather than the diacritics) are [round], [high], [low], [front], and [back]. [labial] is predictable, occurring only in [+round] vowels. All other features are invariant; unless overridden by a diacritic, all vowels are [+syllabic, -consonantal, +sonorant, +continuant, 0delayed release, +approximant, -tap, -trill, -nasal, -voice, -spread glottis, +constricted glottis, -labiodental, -coronal, 0anterior, 0distributed, 0strident].
